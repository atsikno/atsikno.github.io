Intro





Fretboard is the ultimate guitar chord finder and fretboard visualizer. See and hear chords, and tap the dots on the fretboard to hear individual notes from the chord up and down the neck.

Simply tap the chord to quickly change root, type or optional bass note. View fingerings for over 400 chords, each in up to 5 positions.


More Videos

Alternatively, view the root (white dot) and intervals (3rd, 7th, 9th etc) for every chord right across the fretboard. (bass note shown in red if there is one). This allows you to see all the ways you could play a chord at once and gives you options you didn't know existed.

Save chords to a progression so you can quickly flip between chords and see the changing fret positions. Ideal whether you are just starting out or an expert trying to master complex progressions.
