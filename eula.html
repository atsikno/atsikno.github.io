Privacy Policy

Android Apps. Atsikno Ltd (Atsikno): 16th May 2016.

Atsikno apps do not connect to any remote server run by Atsikno and they send no personally identifiable information (PII) to Atsikno. If an error occurs users may be asked to send an error report to Google via the standard Android reporting mechanisms and this may be used by Atsikno to diagnose the fault. No PII is exposed to Atsikno and no information is passed to any third party unless required by law.

Apps that connect to internet web sites connect only to the sites listed in the app and do so via standard http requests as would a web browser. Atsikno is not endorsed by or affiliated with these web sites.

The apps use Google Play Services to enable in-app purchases.

Apps that require the 'Identity' permission have access to your email address but will not send you email. Your email address is used only to form an encrypted token that is used in communication with Google Play Services.

Apps that are ad supported use Google's AdMob service.

Google's privacy policy can be found here.
